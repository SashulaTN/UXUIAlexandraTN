My Story

My name is Alexandra Tolmacheva Nguyen, and I live and work in San Diego, CA. I call myself evolving UX/UI designer. I had a long journey before I’ve got interest and passion about UX/UI design. After graduation from school of Art and Design (Russia), I started my professional career as a print designer. Being always fascinated about technologies, I continuously learned new skills and softwares. In 2004, I moved to United States and worked as print/graphic designer. Even though I enjoyed it, I always wanted to make a leap from designing for print to designing for screen. I started taking a projects and create a landing pages, interactive banners, and learned HTML/CSS. A couple years later, I was hired as UI/Graphic designer at Abgent.

While creating webpages and UI elements, I always thought about logic and structure behind the elements user actually see and interact with. Over time, I got deep interest to UX/UI design and start to learn about UX research and design validation. Soon, I’ve got a chance to work on my first UX/UI project and created a responsive website “MegaPoints” reward program.

As UX design is a process, I am constantly in process to learn about UX. Recently, I’ve got training from Bloc to enrich my skills and knowledges about UX design. Making design based on research and data, makes process meaningful and fascinated to me.

Other Interests

I was always fascinated about human anatomy and facial features, and It helps me to excel in portrait drawing. My favorite media is acrylic, and I draw a portrait on a “wood doll”. I do pay attention to details a lot, and It makes my drawing look like a photography.
